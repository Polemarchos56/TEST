The History of Efstathios Loupas and how I met him

On November 5th, 2021, I met Mr. Efstathios Loupas for the first time. I was a new student in his zoom classroom, and I was immediately struck by his enthusiasm and energy. From the very beginning, it was clear that Mr. Loupas was passionate about teaching and genuinely cared about his students' success.

His teaching style was engaging and interactive, and he always made sure to provide plenty of opportunities for students to ask questions and participate in class discussions.

Over the years, Mr. Loupas has continued to be an inspiring and dedicated teacher, and I can honestly say that he is the best teacher I have ever had. His impact on my education and my life is immeasurable, and I am grateful for the opportunity to have learned from him.
